Plans

Group II (Eye, Dental, Physiotherapy and Alternative Medicine)

Rs.1500/- | Year

Silver Plan
AD MyClinic Software License – 1 Year
No. of New Registrations/Patients – 10
Additional Benefits – Advertisements worth INR 1000/- free
Discounts – 5% discount on AD Advertisement packages for 1 Year

Terms of Plan

Minimum Discount for New Registrations – 50%
Minimum Discount for Revisiting Patients – 10%
Minimum Discount for Revisiting Patients – 10%
AD Referral Share on Subsequent Consultations – 10%

How This Plan Works?

Doctors registering under the Silver Plan shall be required to pay INR 1500/- as the Plan fee. Once this fee is paid, the doctor shall be able to secure 10 new patients. For each new registration, the doctor shall be providing a minimum of 50% discount to the patient. For every subsequent visit of a registered patient, the doctor shall be providing a minimum of 10% discount to the patient. Also in the event of a revisiting patient, the doctor shall be paying 10% of the appointment fee as referral amount to AarogyaData.

What Else Do You Get?

Apart from the regular flow of new patients along with follow-up visits of the already registered ones, you will be able to put up advertisements worth INR 1000/- on AD site. You can also opt for a preferred advertisement package for displaying your ad on AarogyaData site for 1 year and avail a discount of 5% on the package chosen.
